Neo

Neo is the protagonist of The Matrix franchise. A computer hacker who learns from mysterious rebels about the true nature of his reality and his role in the war against its controllers.

Morpheus

Morpheus is a computer programmer and a leader of the rebel forces. He is a mentor to Neo, offering him guidance and wisdom as he learns about the Matrix and his role in the war against the machines.

Trinity

Trinity is a skilled hacker and fighter who is a member of the rebel forces. She is a close ally and romantic interest of Neo, and plays a key role in the fight against the machines.
